# Rapport

Build deep connection and trust through genuine rapport building techniques.

## Understanding Rapport

Rapport is the foundation of all effective influence. It's the feeling of connection, trust, and mutual understanding between people.

## Core Elements

- **Trust Building**: Establishing credibility and reliability
- **Common Ground**: Finding shared experiences and values
- **Active Listening**: Truly hearing and understanding others
- **Mirroring**: Subtle matching of communication styles

## Building Rapport

- Authentic interest in others
- Finding genuine similarities
- Demonstrating understanding
- Creating safe spaces for communication

## Applications

- Sales conversations
- Leadership interactions
- Team building
- Relationship development
