# Identity

Understand how identity shapes decisions and learn to work with identity-level change.

## The Power of Identity

Identity is who people believe themselves to be. It's the strongest driver of behavior and decision-making.

## Key Concepts

- **Self-Concept**: How people see themselves
- **Identity Congruence**: Aligning actions with identity
- **Identity Shifts**: How identity evolves and changes
- **Role Identity**: Different aspects of identity in different contexts

## Working with Identity

- Helping people see new aspects of themselves
- Aligning proposals with existing identity
- Supporting positive identity development
- Recognizing identity conflicts

## Applications

- Personal development
- Organizational change
- Sales and marketing
- Coaching and mentoring
