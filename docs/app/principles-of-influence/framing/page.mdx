# Framing

Master the art of presenting information in the most compelling and effective way.

## What is Framing?

Framing is the way you present information, context, or situations to influence how others perceive and interpret them.

## Key Concepts

- **Context Setting**: Establishing the right environment for your message
- **Perspective Shifting**: Helping others see from different viewpoints
- **Value Highlighting**: Emphasizing the most relevant benefits
- **Problem Presentation**: Framing challenges as opportunities

## Applications

- Presentations and pitches
- Negotiations
- Everyday conversations
- Written communication
